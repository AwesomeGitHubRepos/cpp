# ([C++](Cpp.md)) [std::minmax](CppStdMinmax.md)

[std::minmax](CppStdMinmax.md) is an [algorithm](CppAlgorithm.md) to
obtain the begin and end of a [container](CppContainer.md) at the same
time.
